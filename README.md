SUSTAINABLE RETAIL

Founded in 2023 by Dhanraj Solanki and Hemant Thapa, Costrick is headquartered in Glasgow and is set to transform the retail industry by providing a free, user-friendly Comparison Shopping Engine (CSE) are specifically made for convenience stores. Our mission is to empower small businesses by simplifying their purchasing decisions, saving them time and money, and fostering sustainable practices that benefit both the environment and the local economy.
